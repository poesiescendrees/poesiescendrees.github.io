Canine Breathes

Redefining justice, establishing the era of the wolf

Draw blood, strike first, entangle me with your feral soul
Hypnotic hallucinations, your growl echoes in my ears
The fire in your eyes keeps me guarded from all outside of us
All of them frozen in fear, facing a domestic dog

Turned into a monster so vile, making every wrong right again
Taking back what was stolen, returning control
Redefining justice, establishing the era of the wolf
As long as the canine breathes, none shall go unpunished
