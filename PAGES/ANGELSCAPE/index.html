About

angelscape (Julius Alexei, he/him) is a 22 years old Finnish illusrtator and storyteller. Based in Finland, he has been active on social medias for a few years. He mainly produces numeric art with an undeniable anime aesthetic and has regularly been active in graphic contests and challenges.

Instagram Challenge

Back in November, 2022, Angelscape launched a drawing contest called “Huion And Angel Halloween”. The results were posted five days after Halloween of the same year.

With the sponsor of the tablet label, the artist had the opportunity to start one of the challenges they were so font of. It was based on the “Draw This in Your Style” principle, which welcomes the participants to reinvente a piece of art specially made by the artist in their own style. Angelscape announciated the contest winners on a post in Instagram on November 5, 2022. It had more than 400 entries with multiple artistic styles.
