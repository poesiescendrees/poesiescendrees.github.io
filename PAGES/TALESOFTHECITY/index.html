Margot is one of the 'new generation' at 28, Barbary Lane. She starts the series as the girlfriend of Jake, a trans man and fellow resident of Barbary Lane. However, their relationship quickly gets tough. Margot struggles to come to terms with Jake's transition and how to define her own sexuality. At the same time, she tries to help with the mysteries going around in Barbary Lane, and goes on with her life as a performer at a queer burlesque bar. She and Jake eventually break up, and are on bad terms for a while before making it up in the last episode. During the series Margot meets and starts dating a much older woman, DeDe Halcyon Day. read more
